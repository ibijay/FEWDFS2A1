Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter over a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs with cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the egg from the edge of the pan tothe centre every 20 seconds(as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When eggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
